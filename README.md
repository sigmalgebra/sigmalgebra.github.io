# Welcome to Nello's Italian Cafe 🎓

## Introduction

Welcome to my Italian course! This course is dedicated to my friends who want to learn Italian in a fun and informal way. While this course is more of a joke than a serious endeavor, we will still try to learn something new each time. Get ready to laugh, learn, and maybe enjoy some Italian food!

## About Me

My name is Nello and I am your Italian teacher. I am not a certified teacher, but I love the Italian language and enjoy sharing it with my friends. This course is my way of combining fun with learning.

## Course Structure

### Weekly Lessons

We will meet every week for a lesson on the coffe bar on the 3rd floor. The lessons will last about an hour and cover various topics, including:

- **Basic Vocabulary**: Useful words and phrases for everyday life.
- **Conversation Practice**: Simple dialogue practice.
- **Italian Culture**: Traditions, food, and more.
- **Games and Activities**: To make learning more fun.
